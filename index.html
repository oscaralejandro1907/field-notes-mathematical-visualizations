Scientific Visualizations

An interactive collection of mathematical models and geometric explorations.

Surface Integral: dS over dA

Interactive 3D projection model exploring vector calculus foundations. Visualizes how an infinitesimal patch element dS on a curved surface projects onto dA on the xy-plane region.

Parametric Surface: 3D Torus

Advanced real-time rendering of a parameterized three-dimensional torus using WebGL. Controls integration resolution grid metrics, rotation trajectories, and topological transparency meshes.
